Small status pill. Tones map to the semantic palette — use `success` for "exempt", `warning` for PETs nearing expiry, `danger` for overdue/within-estate, `gold` for premium or completed milestones. `dot` adds a status indicator.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Exempt</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" dot>2 yrs remaining</Badge>
<Badge tone="gold">Premium</Badge>
```
