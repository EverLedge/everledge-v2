Compact gift record: recipient avatar, relationship, value, gift date, PET status badge, and a mini seven-year progress bar. The whole card turns gold once the gift is fully exempt. Use in grids on the Gifts view.

```jsx
<GiftCard recipient="Eleanor Whitcombe" relationship="Daughter"
  value="£250,000" giftDate="2021-04-02" onClick={openGift} />
```
